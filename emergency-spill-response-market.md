<p><p>The emergency spill response market consists of various types of products used to address and mitigate the impact of spills. Skimmers are devices used to remove oil or other liquids from the surface of water. Booms are floating barriers that help contain and control spills. Dispersants and dispersant products break down oil into smaller droplets. Sorbents absorb and contain spills. Transfer products aid in transferring liquids from one container to another. Radio communication products facilitate effective communication during response efforts. Other products include personal protective equipment, spill kits, and containment systems.</p></p>
