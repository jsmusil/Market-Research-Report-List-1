<p><ul><li>Hollow Shaft</li><li>Solid Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parallel shaft geared motors are used in various industries to transmit power and control speed in machines. They come in two types: hollow shaft and solid shaft. Hollow shaft geared motors have an empty center, allowing the user to easily connect and disconnect the motor from the machine. Solid shaft geared motors have a solid, non-removable shaft that directly connects to the machine. Both types offer advantages depending on the application, with hollow shaft motors providing flexibility and ease of installation, while solid shaft motors provide a more secure and direct connection.</p></p>
