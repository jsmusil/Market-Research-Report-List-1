<p><p>Wedge brakes are a type of braking system commonly used in heavy vehicles, such as trucks and trailers. There are two main types of wedge brakes: single chamber and double chamber. Single chamber wedge brakes consist of a single chamber containing a wedge-shaped brake shoe, which expands and applies pressure to the brake drum when the brakes are engaged. On the other hand, double chamber wedge brakes have two separate chambers with individual brake shoes, providing improved braking performance and redundancy in case of a failure in one chamber.</p></p>
