<p><ul><li>Refracting Telescope</li><li>Reflecting Telescope</li><li>Catadioptric Telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are three main types of telescopes in the market: refracting, reflecting, and catadioptric telescopes. Refracting telescopes use a lens to gather and focus light, providing clear images of the sky. Reflecting telescopes use a curved mirror to reflect and gather light, producing high-quality images. Catadioptric telescopes combine both lenses and mirrors to maximize light collection and have compact designs. They are known for their versatility and portability. These different telescope types cater to various needs and preferences of astronomers, offering a range of options in the market.</p></p>
