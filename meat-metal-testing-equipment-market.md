<p><p>Meat metal testing equipment refers to devices used to detect metallic contaminants in meat products. There are two main types of equipment available in the market: metal detectors and X-ray devices. Metal detectors are used to identify metal particles by emitting electromagnetic waves and analyzing the reflection. X-ray devices, on the other hand, use X-ray imaging to detect metallic contaminants in meat products. Both devices play a crucial role in ensuring food safety by identifying and preventing metal contamination in the meat industry.</p></p>
