In the merchant/bulk market, hydrogen is stored and supplied in large quantities to industries and consumers through pipelines or tanker trucks. On-site storage refers to the storing and usage of hydrogen produced on-site for industrial processes and power generation. On-board storage pertains to storing hydrogen in vehicles or transportation systems, enabling them to use hydrogen as a fuel source.</p></p>
