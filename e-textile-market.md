<p><p>The e-textile market can be categorized into three main types: passive electronic textiles, active electronic textiles, and ultra-electronic textiles. Passive electronic textiles are embedded with conductive fabrics or threads that can sense and transmit data without the need for external power. Active electronic textiles incorporate components like sensors, actuators, and power sources to enable functionalities like illumination or temperature regulation. Ultra-electronic textiles go a step further by integrating advanced technologies such as nanotechnology or wireless communication, offering enhanced capabilities like smart sensing, energy harvesting, or biometric monitoring.</p></p>
