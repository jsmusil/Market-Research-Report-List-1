<p><p>Thermal CTP (Computer-to-Plate) market comprises three types: Semi-automatic CTP, Fully automatic CTP, and Manual CTP. Semi-automatic CTP systems involve some manual intervention, such as loading and unloading plates. Fully automatic CTP systems automate the entire plate-making process, from plate loading to unloading, reducing human intervention. Manual CTP systems require complete manual involvement throughout the plate-making process. These different market types cater to diverse user needs in terms of plate-making automation levels, providing options for varying degrees of manual intervention and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
