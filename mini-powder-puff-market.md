<p><p>Mini powder puffs come in two market types: wet puff and dry puff. A wet puff is designed to be used with liquid or creamy products like foundation or BB cream. It has a soft, dense texture that helps blend the product smoothly onto the skin. On the other hand, a dry puff is used with powder-based products like setting powder or blush. It has a slightly looser texture, allowing it to pick up and distribute powder evenly for a flawless finish. Both types serve different purposes and cater to individual preferences in makeup application.</p></p>
