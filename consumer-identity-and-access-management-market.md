<p><p>Consumer Identity and Access Management (CIAM) market can be broadly categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud CIAM solutions are hosted on remote servers, managed by a third-party provider and accessed by organizations over the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. On the other hand, on-premises CIAM solutions are installed locally on an organization's servers and managed by their IT team. They provide more control and customization options but require higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Organizations choose between these types based on their preferences, security requirements, and resource availability.</p></p>
