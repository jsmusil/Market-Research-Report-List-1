<p><p>Stretch adhesive bandages are widely used for medical purposes. There are three main types in the market: roller bandages, tubular bandages, and triangular bandages. Roller bandages are long strips of fabric that can be wrapped around limbs to secure dressings or provide support. Tubular bandages are sleeve-like structures that are slipped over body parts to provide compression and protection. Triangular bandages are triangular-shaped and often used for creating slings or as dressings for head injuries. These different types offer various functionalities to meet different medical needs.</p></p>
