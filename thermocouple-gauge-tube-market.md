<p><p>A thermocouple gauge tube is a type of pressure measuring device used in various industries to measure and monitor vacuum levels. It consists of two dissimilar metal wires, typically made of nickel and chromium alloys, which are welded together at one end to form a thermocouple. The other end of the wires is connected to a gauge that measures the voltage generated by the thermocouple. By measuring this voltage, the pressure or vacuum level can be determined.</p><p>The future outlook for the thermocouple gauge tube market is positive, with significant growth anticipated in the coming years.
