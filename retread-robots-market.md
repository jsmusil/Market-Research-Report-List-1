<p><p>Retread Robots have various market types. Articulated robots have jointed arms which allow flexibility in movement. Cartesian robots have three linear axes that move in a rectangular coordinate system. Cylindrical robots have a rotary base and an arm that moves in a cylindrical coordinate system. Polar robots have a rotating joint at the base and an arm that moves in a spherical coordinate system. SCARA robots have two parallel arms and are used for assembly. Delta robots have a fixed base and an arm that moves using parallelograms, suitable for high-speed applications like pick and place tasks.</p></p>
