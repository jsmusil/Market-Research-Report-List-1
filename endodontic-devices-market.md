<p><p>The endodontic devices market includes two main types of products: instruments and consumables. Instruments are the tools used by dentists during endodontic procedures, such as root canals. These can include items like hand files, rotary files, and apex locators. Consumables, on the other hand, are the materials used during the procedure, such as obturation materials like gutta-percha points and sealer. These products are essential for successful endodontic treatments and are used by dental professionals worldwide.</p></p>
