<p><p>The platinum sputtering target market consists of two main types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. The rotary type market refers to the segment where the target rotates during the sputtering process. This type of target allows for a more even distribution of sputtered material, making it ideal for applications that require high uniformity. On the other hand, the non-rotatable type market involves targets that remain stationary during the sputtering process. These targets are commonly used in applications where uniformity is not a critical factor, such as research and development or smaller-scale production.</p></p>
