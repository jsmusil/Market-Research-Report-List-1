<p><p>Drive-in pallet racking is a type of storage system that maximizes warehouse space by allowing forklifts to directly enter and retrieve pallets. The market types of drive-in pallet racking include duplex, straight-through, and single entry. Duplex type refers to a system where forklifts can enter from one side and exit from the other, enabling a first-in-first-out (FIFO) inventory management system. Straight-through type allows forklifts to enter from one side and exit from the same side, suitable for last-in-first-out (LIFO) inventory management. Single entry type presents a single access point for forklifts to enter and exit the racking system.</p></p>
