<p><p>The Tape Peel Tester market is segmented into four major types: Motor, Hydraulic, Pneumatic, and Electromagnetic. Motor-driven tape peel testers utilize a motor to provide the necessary force for testing. Hydraulic tape peel testers use hydraulic power to generate and control the testing force. Pneumatic tape peel testers employ compressed air to apply the required force for testing. Electromagnetic tape peel testers utilize electromagnetic force systems to achieve accurate and consistent results. These different market types offer various testing options to meet the needs of different industries and applications.</p></p>
