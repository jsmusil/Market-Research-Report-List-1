<p><p>The Aerospace Open Die Forgings market includes three types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging involves the production of unique forgings to meet specific customer requirements. Captive Forging refers to in-house forging facilities established by aerospace companies to ensure a dedicated supply of components. Catalog Forging involves the production of standard forgings that are available for purchase from catalogs, typically used for non-critical applications. These three types cater to different aerospace industry needs, providing flexibility and reliability in forging production.</p></p>
