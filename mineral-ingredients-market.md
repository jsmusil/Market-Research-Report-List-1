<p><p>The mineral ingredients market can be classified into two main types: micronutrients and macronutrients. They play a vital role in various biological processes. Macronutrients, on the other hand, are minerals required in larger quantities. They include minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium, and are crucial for growth, development, and overall health. Both types of mineral ingredients have significant importance in nutrition and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
