<p><p>The 5G processor market consists of two types: the Gigabit LTE Spectrum and the mmWave Spectrum market. The Gigabit LTE Spectrum market focuses on the use of the existing LTE infrastructure to provide fast data transfer speeds close to those of 5G. It enables smooth transition to 5G technology by utilizing advanced LTE features. On the other hand, the mmWave Spectrum market utilizes high-frequency mmWave bands to deliver ultra-fast data rates, but with limited coverage. It is ideal for densely populated areas where high bandwidth is required.</p></p>
