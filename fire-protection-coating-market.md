<p><p>Fire Protection Coatings include Liquid Fire-resistant Coatings and Powder Fire-resistant Coatings. Liquid fire-resistant coatings are applied with a sprayer or brush and are commonly used for steel structures, wood, and other materials. They offer protection by forming a barrier between the material and the fire. Powder fire-resistant coatings are applied as a dry powder and can be used on metal surfaces to provide fire protection. Each type of coating has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
