<p><ul><li>Electronic Thermometer</li><li>Infrared Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The basal thermometer market comprises two main types: electronic thermometers and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers are equipped with a digital display and measure temperature by using sensors to detect electrical resistance changes in a metal probe. Infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature by detecting the thermal radiation emitted from the subject. Both types are commonly used for measuring basal body temperature, with electronic thermometers offering precise readings and infrared thermometers offering non-contact measurement, making them suitable for certain applications.</p></p>
