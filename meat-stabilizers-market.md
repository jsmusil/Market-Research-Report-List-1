<p><p>Meat stabilizers are additives used in the food industry to improve the texture, appearance, and shelf life of meat products. There are several types of meat stabilizers available in the market. Gum, such as guar gum or xanthan gum, provides a smooth and stable texture to meat products. Pectin, a natural thickening agent, enhances the juiciness and tenderness of meat. Carrageenan, derived from seaweed, improves moisture retention in meat products. Gelatin, a protein-based stabilizer, enhances the texture and elasticity of meat. Other types of meat stabilizers include starches, proteins, and phosphates.</p></p>
