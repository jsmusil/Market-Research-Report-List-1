Rubber heads are the most common and affordable option, offering good durability and a natural feel. Mylar heads are more durable and produce a louder sound, making them suitable for live performances. Mesh or silicone heads provide the closest feel to acoustic drums and are preferred by professional drummers for their highly realistic response and quiet operation. These different types cater to musicians with different preferences and playing styles.</p></p>
