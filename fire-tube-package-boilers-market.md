<p><p>The fire-tube package boilers market consists of three types: D-type package boilers, A-type package boilers, and O-type package boilers. D-type package boilers have a horizontal layout with a large steam drum at the top and water drums at the bottom. A-type package boilers have a vertical layout with water tubes extending horizontally from the furnace. O-type package boilers have a symmetrical layout with water tubes forming a circle around the furnace. Each type of boiler has its own advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
