<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Embedded fingerprint modules are used for biometric authentication purposes in various devices. Capacitive fingerprint modules rely on the detection of changes in electrical capacitance to capture fingerprint images. Optical fingerprint modules use light to illuminate the finger, and the reflected light is used to create the image. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules generate high-frequency sound waves to capture a 3D image of the fingerprint. These modules find applications in smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other electronic devices to provide secure and convenient access control.</p></p>
