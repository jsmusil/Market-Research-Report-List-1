<p><p>The lasers for marking market can be classified into four main types. Firstly, the fiber type laser utilizes fiber optics to deliver the laser beam, offering high precision and marking speed. Secondly, CO2 lasers employ a gas mixture to emit a laser beam, ideal for marking organic materials such as wood and plastics. Thirdly, solid-state lasers use a solid medium to deliver the laser beam, providing high energy and reliability for various marking applications. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses different types of lasers, including diode-pumped lasers and ultraviolet lasers, offering unique features for specific marking needs.</p></p>
