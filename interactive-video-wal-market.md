<p><p>The interactive video wall market can be classified into three types: LCD, LED, and DLP. LCD video walls use liquid crystal display technology to create high-resolution images with excellent color accuracy. LED video walls employ light-emitting diodes to deliver bright and vibrant visuals with energy efficiency. DLP video walls use digital light processing technology to produce sharp and smooth images by reflecting light off tiny mirrors. These different technologies cater to varying customer preferences and requirements for interactive video wall solutions.</p></p>
