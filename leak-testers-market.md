<p><p>Leak testers are devices used to detect and measure leaks in various products or systems. There are three main types of leak testers: portable, compact, and stationary leak testers. </p><p>Portable leak testers are small and lightweight devices that can be easily transported and operated in different locations. They are convenient for conducting leak tests on-site or in the field. </p><p>Compact leak testers are slightly larger than portable ones but still offer easy mobility. They are specifically designed for applications that require frequent leak testing in limited spaces or production lines. </p><p>Stationary leak testers are larger and more powerful devices. They are typically utilized in industrial settings where continuous and high-volume leak testing is necessary. These leak testers are often integrated into automated production lines and offer advanced features for accurate and efficient leak detection.</p></p>
