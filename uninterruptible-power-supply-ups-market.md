The UPS market can be classified into two types: DC Power Supply and AC Power Supply market. The DC Power Supply market refers to UPS systems that convert incoming AC power into DC power, typically used in data centers and telecommunications. On the other hand, the AC Power Supply market focuses on UPS systems that store power in batteries and convert it back to AC power when needed, commonly used in homes and offices.</p></p>
