<p><p>The HVAC air ducts market comprises three main types of ducts: sheet metal ducts, flexible non-metallic ducts, and fiberglass ducts. Sheet metal ducts are made of metal sheets and are commonly used in larger commercial buildings. Flexible non-metallic ducts are made of plastic or rubber and are preferred for residential applications due to their flexibility and ease of installation. Fiberglass ducts, made of fiberglass-reinforced plastic, offer excellent thermal insulation properties and are energy-efficient, making them suitable for a wide range of applications in both residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
