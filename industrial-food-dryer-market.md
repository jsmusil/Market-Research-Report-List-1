<p><p>The Industrial Food Dryer market is comprised of various types of dryers, each with its unique method of removing moisture from food products. Spray Dryers use hot air to vaporize liquid content, Freeze Dryers freeze products and then remove moisture through sublimation, Fluidized-bed Dryers suspend particles in an upward-moving gas stream for quick drying, Drum Dryers rotate and apply heat to a rotating drum to evaporate moisture, Vacuum Dryers reduce pressure to lower the boiling point of water, and Tray Dryers use heated trays to remove moisture from products placed on them.</p></p>
