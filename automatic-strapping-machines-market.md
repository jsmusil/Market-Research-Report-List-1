<p><p>Automatic strapping machines are used to securely fasten packages and bundles with straps. There are two types of automatic strapping machines in the market: semi-automatic and fully automatic. </p><p>Semi-automatic strapping machines require manual input to feed the strap into the machine and to start and stop the strapping process. They are suitable for low to medium volume applications. </p><p>Fully automatic strapping machines, on the other hand, operate without manual input once the straps are loaded. They are ideal for high-volume applications and provide increased efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
