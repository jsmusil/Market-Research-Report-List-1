<p><p>Miniature thermopile detectors are used in various applications to detect temperature changes and convert them into electrical signals. The market for these detectors is categorized into two types: circular window format and square window format. Circular window format detectors have a circular-shaped window that allows for a wider field of view and uniform sensitivity. Square window format detectors have a square-shaped window that provides a higher signal-to-noise ratio and is ideal for applications requiring precise measurements. Both formats offer different advantages depending on the specific requirements and are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
