<p><p>The Plastic Cap Capping Machine market can be categorized into two main types: Semi-automatic and Automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention to some extent throughout the capping process. They are generally suitable for smaller production capacities and require operators to place the caps manually before the machine seals them. On the other hand, Automatic machines are fully automated and can handle higher production volumes with minimal manual involvement. These machines can automatically feed, seal, and even sort the caps, ensuring more efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
