<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) submersible pumps are water pumps that are powered by solar energy. They are designed to be submerged in water, usually in wells or boreholes, to pump water to the surface. These pumps are commonly used in agricultural and irrigation systems, as well as in remote areas where there is no access to electricity. On the other hand, surface pumps are water pumps that are not submerged and are usually installed above ground level. They are used to draw water from a water source like a river or a lake and are commonly used for domestic purposes or in small-scale irrigation systems.</p></p>
