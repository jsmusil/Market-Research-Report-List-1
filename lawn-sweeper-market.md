<p><strong>The Lawn Sweeper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-along Sweepers</li><li>Tow-behind Sweeper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lawn sweeper market can be categorized into two types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are manually operated and require the user to push them along the lawn to collect debris. They are suitable for small to medium-sized lawns. On the other hand, tow-behind sweepers are attached to a riding mower or tractor and are suitable for larger lawns or commercial purposes. They are pulled behind the machinery and collect debris efficiently as the vehicle moves forward. Both types of sweepers are designed to make the task of collecting leaves, grass clippings, and other debris from the lawn easier and more efficient.</p></p>
