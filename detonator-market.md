<p><p>Detonators have various market types, including industrial electric detonators, shock tube detonators, and others. Industrial electric detonators are widely used in mining and construction industries for controlled explosions. They are triggered by an electric current to initiate the explosion. Shock tube detonators are commonly used in military applications and seismic exploration. They utilize a physical shock wave to transmit the initiation signal. The "others" category typically includes non-electric or specialized detonators tailored for specific purposes, such as safety fuse detonators or programmable delay detonators. These different types cater to diverse needs and safety requirements in explosive applications.</p></p>
