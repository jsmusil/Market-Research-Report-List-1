<p><p>The Rollover Protection Accessory market comprises three types: Cutside Car Protection, Inside Car Protection, and Skeleton Protection. Cutside Car Protection refers to external roll bars or cages installed on the sides of a vehicle, offering additional structural support during a rollover event. Inside Car Protection includes features like reinforced roofs, seatbelt pretensioners, and airbags designed to protect occupants within the vehicle. Skeleton Protection refers to a framework integrated into the vehicle's structure, providing an integrated rollover protection system. Each type serves a vital role in enhancing safety during rollover accidents.</p></p>
