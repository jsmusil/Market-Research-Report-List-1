<p><p>Universal temperature transmitters are devices used to convert temperature measurements into electrical signals that can be easily processed and transmitted. Two common types of universal temperature transmitters are head mount and DIN rail mount. Head mount transmitters are directly installed on the temperature sensor's connection head, providing a compact and convenient solution. DIN rail mount transmitters, on the other hand, are mounted onto the DIN rail, allowing for easy installation and maintenance in control panels or cabinets. Both types offer reliable temperature measurement and compatibility with various temperature sensors.</p></p>
