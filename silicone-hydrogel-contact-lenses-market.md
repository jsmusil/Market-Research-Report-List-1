<p><strong>The Silicone Hydrogel Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Form</li><li>Toric Form</li><li>Multifocal Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicone Hydrogel Contact Lenses market can be divided into three types - spherical form, toric form, and multifocal form. Spherical form lenses are designed to correct common vision problems like myopia (nearsightedness) and hyperopia (farsightedness). Toric form lenses are specially shaped to correct astigmatism, providing clear and distortion-free vision. Multifocal form lenses are intended for individuals with presbyopia, a condition that affects near vision in middle-aged and older adults. They provide different optical zones for clear vision at various distances, eliminating the need for reading glasses.</p></p>
