This integration provides a more comprehensive analysis by combining the strengths of different methods, such as XPS with scanning electron microscopy (SEM) or XPS with time-of-flight secondary ion mass spectrometry (TOF-SIMS). The integrated systems offer enhanced capabilities and improved efficiency, making them highly desirable in research and industrial applications.</p><p>Another trend is the miniaturization of XPS instruments.
