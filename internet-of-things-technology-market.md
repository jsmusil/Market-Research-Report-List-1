<p><p>Internet of Things (IoT) technology refers to the network of physical devices, vehicles, appliances, and other objects embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity to enable them to collect and exchange data. The increasing adoption of IoT across industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, transportation, agriculture, and retail is driving market growth. The integration of IoT with artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud computing is further fueling the expansion of the market.</p><p>Additionally, the proliferation of connected devices and the widespread availability of high-speed internet are creating a favorable environment for the growth of IoT technology. The increasing demand for real-time data analytics and the need for improved operational efficiency are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, edge computing is gaining prominence in the IoT technology market. Edge computing enables data processing and analytics to be performed closer to the source, reducing latency and enabling faster decision-making. Edge devices are becoming more sophisticated, enabling more complex data analysis at the edge, which enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of IoT systems.</p><p>Moreover, the convergence of IoT with 5G technology is expected to bring significant advancements to the market.
