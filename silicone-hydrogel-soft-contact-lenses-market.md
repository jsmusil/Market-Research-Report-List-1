<p><p>Silicone hydrogel soft contact lenses are a type of contact lens made from a combination of hydrogels and silicone materials. Compared to traditional hydrogel lenses, silicone hydrogel lenses have a higher oxygen transmissibility, allowing more oxygen to reach the cornea, resulting in improved comfort and eye health. These lenses also offer better resistance to dehydration and increased flexibility.</p><p>The silicone hydrogel soft contact lenses market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing prevalence of vision-related disorders, such as myopia and hypermetropia, along with the growing aging population, has been driving the demand for contact lenses. Additionally, the rising awareness about eye care, advancements in lens technology, and the desire for improved overall visual experience have contributed to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also positively impacted the silicone hydrogel soft contact lenses market. The increased adoption of contact lenses due to their convenience and reduced dependency on eyeglasses has been witnessed, leading to a surge in market demand.</p><p>In terms of latest trends, the market is witnessing a growing demand for daily disposable silicone hydrogel lenses. These lenses offer enhanced convenience, hygiene, and comfort, as users can simply discard them after each use. Moreover, the advent of online sales channels and direct-to-consumer marketing strategies have made silicone hydrogel lenses more accessible to consumers, further propelling market growth.</p><p>In summary, the silicone hydrogel soft contact lenses market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12% during the forecast period. Factors such as the rising prevalence of vision-related disorders, increasing aging population, advancements in lens technology, and the adoption of contact lenses as an alternative to eyeglasses are driving market growth.
