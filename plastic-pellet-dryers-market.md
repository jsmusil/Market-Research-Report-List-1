<p><p>Plastic pellet dryers are machines used in the plastic manufacturing industry to remove moisture from plastic pellets. There are two main types of plastic pellet dryers: batch dryers and continuous dryers. </p><p>Batch dryers are designed to dry a specific quantity of pellets in a single batch. These dryers are typically used for smaller production runs or when different types of plastics need to be dried separately.</p><p>On the other hand, continuous dryers are designed to dry plastic pellets continuously, allowing for a constant and uninterrupted production process. These dryers are often used in large-scale plastic manufacturing facilities where high production volumes are required.</p></p>
