<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metalcasting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalcasting is a diverse market with various types of casting processes. Gravity casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold, relying on gravity for shape formation. High-pressure die casting uses high pressure to force molten metal into a mold cavity. Low-pressure die casting is similar, but with lower pressure. Sand casting employs a mold made from a mixture of sand and a binder, where the molten metal is poured into the mold cavity. Each casting process offers unique advantages and is utilized in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
