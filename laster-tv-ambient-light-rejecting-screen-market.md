<p><p>A Laster TV Ambient Light Rejecting (ALR) screen is a type of screen designed specifically for use with laser projectors. It is designed to enhance picture quality by minimizing the impact of ambient light in the room, resulting in a clearer and more vibrant image. ALR screens achieve this by reflecting the projected light directly back to the viewer while absorbing ambient light from other directions.</p><p>In recent years, the market for Laster TV Ambient Light Rejecting screens has experienced significant growth. The increasing popularity of home theater systems and the rising demand for improved picture quality have been driving factors in this growth. Additionally, the rising adoption of laser projectors in commercial applications such as boardrooms and classrooms has also contributed to the market expansion.</p><p>One major trend in the Laster TV Ambient Light Rejecting Screen market is the increasing preference for larger screen sizes.
