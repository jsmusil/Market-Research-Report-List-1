<p><p>The shoe making machine market consists of two main types: full automatic and semi-automatic machines. Full automatic machines are highly efficient and perform all the shoe making processes automatically, from cutting leather to stitching and assembling. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention in certain stages of the shoe making process. While full automatic machines are faster and more expensive, semi-automatic machines offer a more affordable option for manufacturers with smaller-scale production needs. Both types cater to the diverse demands and budgets of shoe manufacturers in the market.</p></p>
