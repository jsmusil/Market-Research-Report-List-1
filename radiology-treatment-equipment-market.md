<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiology treatment equipment market includes three main types: External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, and Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment. External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment utilizes high-energy rays to destroy cancer cells from outside the body, while Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment involves the implantation of radioactive sources directly into or near the tumor. Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment administers radioactive substances orally or intravenously throughout the body to target cancer cells. These equipment types play crucial roles in delivering effective radiation treatment to patients with various types of cancer.</p></p>
