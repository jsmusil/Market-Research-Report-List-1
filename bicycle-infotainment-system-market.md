<p><p>The Bicycle Infotainment System market consists of two main types: Wearable Kits and On-Board Kits. Wearable Kits are designed to be worn by the cyclist and provide information such as speed, distance, and navigation directly to the user. They are typically in the form of smartwatches or other wearable devices. On the other hand, On-Board Kits are installed on the bicycle itself and offer features like GPS, music playback, and ride analytics. They enhance the overall cycling experience by providing real-time data and entertainment options.</p></p>
