<p><p>Switch mode power transformers are widely used in electronic devices to convert one voltage level to another. They are categorized into two types: single-excited and double-excited. The single-excited market refers to the transformers where the primary winding is excited by a single-source voltage. These transformers are simpler and cost-effective. On the other hand, the double-excited market consists of transformers with dual primary windings, excited by two separate sources. This type allows for improved efficiency, reduced losses, and better control in applications that require higher power outputs. Both types serve specific purposes based on the desired power requirements and cost considerations.</p></p>
