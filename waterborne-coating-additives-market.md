<p><strong>The Waterborne Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wetting & Dispersion Additives</li><li>Defoaming Agents</li><li>Rheology Modifiers</li><li>Flow Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterborne coating additives are essential components in the coating industry to enhance the performance of water-based coatings. Wetting & Dispersion Additives are used to improve pigment dispersion, resulting in a smooth and uniform coating. Defoaming Agents help to eliminate foam formation during the coating process. Rheology Modifiers control the flow and leveling properties of the coating, while Flow Additives improve application properties and reduce surface defects. Each type of additive plays a crucial role in achieving high-quality and durable waterborne coatings.</p></p>
