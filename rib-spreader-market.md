<p><p>A rib spreader is a surgical instrument used in thoracic surgeries, specifically to spread or separate the ribs and allow access to the chest cavity. It is designed to minimize damage to surrounding tissues and provide adequate space for the surgical procedure. Rib spreaders are widely used in surgeries such as heart bypass, lung resection, and implantation of medical devices.</p><p>The rib spreader market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of lung diseases, cardiovascular disorders, and the rising number of surgeries related to these conditions. Advancements in healthcare infrastructure, growing awareness about early diagnosis and treatment, and the development of minimally invasive surgical techniques are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing geriatric population and higher demand for effective surgical procedures are further propelling market growth. The rib spreader market is also being driven by technological advancements, such as the introduction of 3D-printed rib spreaders and the adoption of robotic-assisted surgeries.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards minimally invasive techniques, which require smaller and specialized rib spreaders. This enables surgeons to perform precise and less invasive surgeries, resulting in reduced post-operative complications and faster recovery times for patients. Furthermore, there is a growing demand for rib spreaders with adjustable blades and ergonomic designs to enhance surgical accuracy and ease of use.</p><p>Overall, the rib spreader market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. The market's growth can be attributed to various factors, including the increasing prevalence of thoracic diseases, advancements in surgical techniques, and the development of innovative rib spreading instruments.</p></p>
