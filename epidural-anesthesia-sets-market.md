<p><p>Epidural anesthesia sets are medical devices used to administer pain relief during surgery or childbirth. Regular epidural sets consist of a catheter inserted into the epidural space of the spine, allowing for continuous medication delivery. Combined spinal epidural sets combine the benefits of both spinal and epidural anesthesia. They include a spinal needle for initial medication injection into the cerebrospinal fluid and an epidural catheter for continuous pain relief. Both types of sets offer different options for administering anesthesia depending on the specific needs of the procedure or patient.</p></p>
