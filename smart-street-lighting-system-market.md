<p><p>The Smart Street Lighting System market comprises two main types: the Wired Street Lighting System and the Wireless Street Lighting System. In a Wired Street Lighting System, the street lights are connected to a central control system through physical cables, allowing for centralized control and monitoring. On the other hand, a Wireless Street Lighting System utilizes wireless communication technologies such as Wi-Fi or cellular networks to control and monitor the street lights remotely. Both systems aim to improve energy efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and enhance overall street lighting management.</p></p>
