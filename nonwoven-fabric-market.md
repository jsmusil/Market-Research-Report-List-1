<p><strong>The Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spun-Bonded Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Needle-Punched Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Spiny Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nonwoven fabric is categorized into various types based on its manufacturing process and properties. Spun-bonded nonwoven fabric is made by bonding filaments together through heat or chemicals, resulting in a smooth and durable material. Needle-punched nonwoven fabric is created by interlocking fibers using barbed needles, providing strength and resilience. Spiny nonwoven fabric features a unique three-dimensional structure with spiky protrusions, offering enhanced filtration capabilities. Lastly, the "others" category includes nonwoven fabrics that are made using different techniques or possess specific characteristics not covered by the aforementioned types, such as melt-blown nonwovens or laminated nonwovens.</p></p>
