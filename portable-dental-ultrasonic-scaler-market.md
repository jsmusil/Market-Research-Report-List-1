<p><strong>The Portable Dental Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sonic Scalers</li><li>Magnetostrictive Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable dental ultrasonic scaler market consists of three main types: sonic scalers, magnetostrictive scalers, and piezoelectric scalers. Sonic scalers use a vibrating tip to remove plaque and tartar buildup, offering gentle cleaning. Magnetostrictive scalers use magnetic pulses to create vibrations, providing efficient and deep cleaning. Piezoelectric scalers utilize a ceramic tip that vibrates under electric current, allowing for accurate and precise cleaning. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different dental needs and preferences.</p></p>
