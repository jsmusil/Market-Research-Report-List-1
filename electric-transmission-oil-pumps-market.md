<p><ul><li>Integrated Pump</li><li>Separate Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated pump in electric transmission oil pumps market refers to the type of pump that is directly attached to the transmission system, sharing the same housing and components. This design allows for a more compact and efficient system. On the other hand, separate pump refers to a pump that is independent from the transmission system, providing more flexibility in design and installation. While integrated pumps are more common due to their compact design, separate pumps are preferred in certain applications for their versatility.</p></p>
