<p><p>Linseed, also known as flaxseed, is available in two market types: whole grain and grated grain. Whole grain linseed refers to the unprocessed form, where the seeds are in their natural state. It retains all the nutrients, fiber, and healthy fats. On the other hand, grated grain linseed is the whole grain linseed that has been finely grated. This type is preferred by some consumers who find it easier to incorporate grated linseed into their meals, such as adding it to smoothies or sprinkling it on salads or cereal. Both market types offer different options for consumers looking to incorporate linseed into their diet.</p></p>
