<p><ul><li>1-channel</li><li>2-channel</li><li>3-channel</li><li>7-channel</li><li>12-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless Holter monitors market includes different types based on the number of channels they offer for recording heart activity. A 1-channel Holter monitor records the heart's electrical signals from a single lead, while a 2-channel monitor captures these signals from two different leads. Similarly, 3-channel monitors have three leads, 7-channel monitors have seven leads, and 12-channel monitors have twelve leads. These various channel options allow healthcare professionals to choose the appropriate Holter monitor based on the complexity of the patient's heart condition and the level of detail required for accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
