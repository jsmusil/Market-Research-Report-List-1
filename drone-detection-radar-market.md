<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The drone detection radar market consists of various types used for detecting and tracking drones. FMCW radar (Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave) operates by continuously transmitting and receiving signals to measure the drone's range and velocity. Pulse-Doppler radar, on the other hand, transmits short-duration pulses and analyzes the frequency shift of the returned signal to determine the drone's speed and distance. Besides, there are other radar technologies employed in the market, each utilizing unique mechanisms to identify drones. These include radar systems based on phased array antennas, passive radar, and holographic radar, among others.</p></p>
