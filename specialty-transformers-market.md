Amorphous dry type transformers use a no-lamination technology to minimize energy losses, making them highly efficient. Epoxy resin casting transformers are made by immersing windings in epoxy resin, providing excellent mechanical strength and protection against moisture and contaminants. Both types offer advantages in terms of reliability, safety, and environmental friendliness, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
