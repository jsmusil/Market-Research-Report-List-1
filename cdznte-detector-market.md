<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CdZnTe detectors are used for various applications such as medical imaging, security screening, and nuclear spectroscopy. The market types for CdZnTe detectors are as follows:</p><p>1. Pixelated: These detectors have an array of small pixels, allowing for high spatial resolution and imaging capabilities.</p><p>2. Coplanar Grid: In these detectors, the electrode configuration is such that it enhances energy resolution and minimizes electrical noise.</p><p>3. Planar-Type: This type is characterized by a large planar surface area, providing good efficiency for gamma-ray detection.</p><p>4. Quasi-Hemispherical: These detectors are designed with a curved surface, which increases the solid angle coverage for incoming radiation.</p><p>5. Housed Quasi-Hemispherical: Similar to quasi-hemispherical detectors, these are housed in protective casings, making them suitable for rugged environments and field applications.</p><p>These different market types cater to specific requirements in terms of resolution, energy efficiency, and environmental durability.</p></p>
