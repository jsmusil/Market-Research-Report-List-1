<p><p>Salinity refractometers are used to measure the salt content in liquid samples. There are two main types: Analog Salinity Refractometer and Digital Salinity Refractometer. The Analog Salinity Refractometer uses a traditional analog scale and requires the user to visually read the measurements. On the other hand, the Digital Salinity Refractometer provides precise digital readings, eliminating the need for manual interpretation.
