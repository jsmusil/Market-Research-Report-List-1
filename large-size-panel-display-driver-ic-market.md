<p><p>The Large Size Panel Display Driver IC Market consists of two main types: Source Drive IC and Gate Drive IC. The Source Drive IC is responsible for controlling the voltage applied to the pixels, determining their brightness and color. On the other hand, the Gate Drive IC manages the circuitry that controls the flow of data into the pixels, allowing for the display of images and videos. These two types of ICs work together to ensure the smooth and accurate functioning of large panel displays.</p></p>
