<p><p>Car subwoofers come in two main types: Powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers have a built-in amplifier, making them easy to install and set up. They are generally smaller in size and more compact. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to power them. They are larger and can handle more power, providing a deeper and more impactful bass response. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the car audio market.</p></p>
