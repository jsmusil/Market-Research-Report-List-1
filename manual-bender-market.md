<p><p>Manual benders are categorized into two main market types: mechanical manual bending machines and electric manual bending machines. Mechanical manual bending machines are operated by manually turning a handle or wheel to bend materials such as metal or plastic. They are suitable for small-scale and low-volume bending tasks. Electric manual bending machines, on the other hand, utilize electricity to power the bending process, making them more efficient and suitable for larger-scale and high-volume bending tasks. Both types serve specific purposes and are chosen based on the nature and scale of the bending job.</p></p>
