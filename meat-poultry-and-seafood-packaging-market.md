Several factors are driving this growth, including the increasing demand for convenience foods, rising disposable income, changing consumer preferences towards processed and ready-to-eat food products, and the need for extended shelf life of perishable food items.</p><p>Additionally, the growing population, urbanization, and the rising number of working women have led to an increased reliance on packaged food products, further fueling the demand for meat, poultry, and seafood packaging. MAP technology removes oxygen and replaces it with a blend of gases, such as nitrogen and carbon dioxide, to reduce spoilage and maintain product freshness.</p><p>In conclusion, the meat, poultry, and seafood packaging market is experiencing robust growth due to the increasing demand for convenience foods, changing consumer preferences, and the need for food safety.
