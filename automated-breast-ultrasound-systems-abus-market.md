<p><p>Automated Breast Ultrasound Systems (ABUS) and Automated Breast Volume Scanner (ABVS) are types of ultrasound machines specifically designed for breast imaging. ABUS uses automated technology to acquire multiple ultrasound images of the entire breast, providing a 3D image for screening and diagnosing breast abnormalities. On the other hand, ABVS is a type of ABUS that focuses on capturing breast volume data. Both systems are used as adjuncts to mammography and can aid in detecting breast cancer in women with dense breast tissue.</p></p>
