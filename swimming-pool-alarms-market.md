<p><p>The swimming pool alarms market consists of various types of alarms designed to enhance safety in swimming pools. Surface wave detection pool alarms are installed around the pool's perimeter and activate when they detect water disturbances. Sub-surface detection pool alarms use sensors submerged under the water's surface to detect any unusual movements. Pool immersion alarms are worn by swimmers and sound an alert when they remain submerged for too long. Pool gate alarms are installed on gates and alert when they are opened. Other types of pool alarms include wearable devices and advanced motion-sensing systems.</p></p>
