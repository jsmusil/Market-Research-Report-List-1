<p><p>A hopper cone is a funnel-shaped bottom structure that is typically used in grain bins or silos for the easy discharge and flow of materials such as grains, seeds, and powders. Additionally, the need for efficient material flow and discharge systems to reduce wastage and optimize storage capacity is fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in hopper cone designs and materials are further propelling the market. Manufacturers are introducing innovative features such as anti-bridging mechanisms, automatic discharge systems, and corrosion-resistant materials to enhance the functionality and durability of hopper cones.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising adoption of automation and control systems in grain storage facilities is creating opportunities for market growth.
