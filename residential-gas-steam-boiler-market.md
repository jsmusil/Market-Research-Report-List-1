<p><p>The residential gas steam boiler market includes two types: multiple steam units and single steam units. Multiple steam units consist of two or more independent boilers connected to a common steam main, allowing for increased capacity and redundancy. This type is suitable for large residential buildings or homes with high heating demands. On the other hand, a single steam unit comprises a single boiler that can adequately meet the heating needs of smaller residential properties with lower demand for steam.</p></p>
