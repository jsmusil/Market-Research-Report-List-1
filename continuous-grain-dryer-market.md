<p><p>Continuous grain dryers are used in the agricultural industry to remove moisture from grains in a continuous and efficient manner. There are two types of continuous grain dryers: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal continuous grain dryers are designed with a horizontal top-loading chamber, allowing for easy loading and unloading of grains. Vertical continuous grain dryers, on the other hand, have a vertical design, allowing for more efficient use of space. Both types of dryers are used to improve the quality and longevity of harvested grains.</p></p>
