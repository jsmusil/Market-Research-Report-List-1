<p><p>A sensor patch, also known as a smart patch or wearable device, is a small electronic module that is attached to the human body to monitor and provide real-time data on various physiological parameters. Sensor patches are widely used in healthcare applications, including continuous monitoring, disease management, and rehabilitation.</p><p>The sensor patch market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 15.00% during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for remote patient monitoring devices, especially in the healthcare sector. Sensor patches enable continuous monitoring of vital signs, such as heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, and glucose levels. This allows healthcare professionals to remotely monitor patients and provide timely interventions, leading to improved patient outcomes and reduced healthcare costs.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as diabetes and cardiovascular disorders, is fueling the adoption of sensor patches.
