<p><p>The home care chemicals market comprises various types of chemicals that are used in the manufacturing of home care products. Surfactants are chemicals that help in reducing surface tension and enhance the cleaning ability of products like detergents. Solvents are substances that dissolve other chemicals to create cleaning solutions. Additives are chemicals added to enhance the performance or appearance of home care products. Pigments are used for coloring products like detergents. "Others" category includes various chemicals like enzymes, fragrances, and preservatives that are used for specific purposes in home care products.</p></p>
