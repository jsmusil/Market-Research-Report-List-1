<p><p>The medical non-woven gauze market can be divided into two types: sterile and non-sterile. Non-sterile gauze, on the other hand, does not have a guarantee of being free from microorganisms and is commonly used for general cleaning purposes. Both types serve vital roles in medical settings, but sterile gauze ensures a higher level of hygiene and is crucial for sterile procedures.</p></p>
