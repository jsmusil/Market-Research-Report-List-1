<p><p>The automotive glass repair fluid market can be categorized into two main types: scratch repair and crack repair. Scratch repair fluids are used to remove and minimize the appearance of superficial scratches on automotive glass surfaces. These fluids typically contain abrasive materials that gently polish the glass, improving its clarity and reducing the visibility of scratches. On the other hand, crack repair fluids are designed to repair small cracks in automotive glass. These fluids typically consist of resin or adhesive materials that are injected into the cracks, helping to prevent further spreading and restoring the structural integrity of the glass.</p></p>
