<p><ul><li>Hydraulically Driven Precision Planter</li><li>Pneumatic Driven Precision Planter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Precision Planter market can be divided into two types: hydraulically driven and pneumatic driven. The hydraulically driven precision planter is powered by a hydraulic system, which allows for precise seed placement and depth control. It is typically used in larger farming operations and offers high accuracy and efficiency. On the other hand, the pneumatic driven precision planter utilizes air pressure to control the seed distribution and planting depth. It is commonly used in smaller farms and offers good accuracy and versatility.</p></p>
