<p><p>Process spectrometers market is segmented into different types based on technology. Near Infrared Type spectrometers analyze samples by measuring the absorption of light in the near infrared region. Fourier-Transform Infrared Type spectrometers use interferometers to analyze the sample. Raman Type spectrometers use scattered light to provide information about molecular vibrations. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Type spectrometers analyze the sample by measuring the interaction of atomic nuclei with a strong magnetic field. Other types include UV-Vis, mass spectrometers, and fluorescence spectroscopy.</p></p>
