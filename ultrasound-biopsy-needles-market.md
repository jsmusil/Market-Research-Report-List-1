<p><strong>The Ultrasound Biopsy Needles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EUS-FNA</li><li>EUS-FNB</li><li>Others(EBUS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound biopsy needles market consists of different types, namely EUS-FNA, EUS-FNB, and Others (EBUS). EUS-FNA refers to Endoscopic Ultrasound-Guided Fine Needle Aspiration, which involves using a thin needle to extract fluid or cells for examination. EUS-FNB stands for Endoscopic Ultrasound-Guided Fine Needle Biopsy, which uses a slightly larger needle to obtain tissue samples. Others (EBUS) includes a different type of ultrasound biopsy called Endobronchial Ultrasound, primarily used for sampling lymph nodes in the chest. These different types cater to the specific needs and preferences of medical professionals performing ultrasound-guided biopsies.</p></p>
