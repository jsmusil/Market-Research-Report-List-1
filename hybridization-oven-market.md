<p><p>The hybridization oven market offers three different types: ordinary, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Ordinary hybridization ovens are basic models that require manual control and monitoring throughout the hybridization process. Semi-automatic models feature some automated functions, such as temperature control, but still require manual intervention for loading and unloading samples. Fully automatic hybridization ovens are equipped with advanced features like automated sample handling and complete control over the hybridization process. These models minimize the need for manual involvement, making them more efficient and convenient for users.</p></p>
