<p><p>The Gemological Instruments Market is categorized into three types of instruments: refractometer, faceting machine, and tester. A refractometer measures the refractive index of gemstones, helping gemologists determine their authenticity and quality. Faceting machines are used to cut and shape gemstones, allowing gemologists to enhance their brilliance and optical properties. Testers assess the authenticity of gemstones by analyzing their electrical and thermal conductivity. These instruments play a crucial role in gemological research, identification, and evaluation, enabling professionals to make accurate judgments and ensure the value and integrity of gemstones.</p></p>
