<p><p>The Hypersensitivity Pneumonitis Treatment Market is categorized into three types based on the duration of symptoms: Acute, Subacute, and Chronic. Acute hypersensitivity pneumonitis occurs within a few hours or days after exposure to the triggering substance. Subacute hypersensitivity pneumonitis develops over weeks or months with worsening symptoms. Chronic hypersensitivity pneumonitis is characterized by long-term inflammation and fibrosis of the lung tissue, developing over years of exposure. Each type requires different treatment approaches and management strategies to alleviate symptoms and prevent further lung damage.</p></p>
