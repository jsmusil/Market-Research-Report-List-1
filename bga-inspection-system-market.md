<p><p>The BGA inspection system market consists of three main types: electrical testing, optical or visual inspection, and X-ray inspection. Electrical testing involves checking the electrical connections and functionality of the BGA components. Optical or visual inspection relies on visual examination to detect any visible defects or flaws in the BGA chips. Lastly, X-ray inspection utilizes X-ray technology to reveal internal defects, such as hidden solder cracks or voids, for a more in-depth inspection. These methods help ensure the quality and reliability of BGA components in various industries.</p></p>
