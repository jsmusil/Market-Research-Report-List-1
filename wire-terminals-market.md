<p><p>The wire terminals market consists of various types of connectors used in electrical and electronic applications. Push-on terminals allow for quick installation and removal without the need for tools. Butt splice connectors are used to join two wires together in line. Ring terminals have a circular shape and are used for securing wires to a screw or stud. Fork terminals have a fork-like shape and are used for easy and secure connections. Bullet terminals have a cylindrical shape and are commonly used in automotive applications.</p></p>
