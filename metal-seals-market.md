<p><p>The metal seals market consists of various types of seals including metal C-Ring, metal E-Ring, metal O-Ring, metal U-Ring, metal W-Ring, and others. Metal C-Rings are designed in a circular shape with a gap, providing flexibility and allowing for easy installation. Metal E-Rings are similar but have a larger gap, offering greater elasticity. Metal O-Rings are round seals, typically used in static applications to prevent leaks. Metal U-Rings have a U-shape and are used for sealing rod or piston applications. Metal W-Rings provide superior stability and are suitable for high-pressure environments. Other metal seals encompass different designs catering to specific sealing requirements.</p></p>
