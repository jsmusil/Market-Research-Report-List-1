<p><p>A multi-band booster is a telecommunications device used to improve the performance and coverage of cellular networks. <p><p>The multi-band booster market is divided into several types, namely dual band, tri-band, quad-band, and five-band boosters. Dual band boosters operate on two frequency bands, typically covering the main cellular frequencies. Tri-band boosters add an additional frequency band to cover more cellular networks and improve signal quality. Quad-band boosters further increase the supported frequency bands, enabling better coverage for various networks. Finally, five-band boosters extend the frequency range even more, ensuring enhanced signal strength and coverage across multiple networks, including 4G and 5G technologies.</p></p>
