<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Optical Metrology market encompasses various types of technologies used for analyzing and measuring different parameters. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) is a method that utilizes chemical reactions to detect and measure specific molecules within a sample. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) utilizes fluorescent markers to measure the presence of certain molecules. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) measures molecules by utilizing enzymes that produce a measurable signal. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) employs radioactive substances to measure specific molecules. These technologies offer efficient and automated solutions for precise measurements in various industries.</p></p>
