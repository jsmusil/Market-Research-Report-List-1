<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pitch Control System</li><li>Electric Pitch Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pitch control systems are essential for optimizing the efficiency and performance of wind turbines. Hydraulic pitch control systems utilize hydraulic cylinders to adjust the angle of the turbine blades, ensuring optimal wind capture. Conversely, electric pitch control systems make use of electric motors to achieve the same purpose. Both systems are designed to respond to changes in wind speed and direction, allowing wind turbines to consistently operate at their maximum output and enhance their overall energy production.</p></p>
