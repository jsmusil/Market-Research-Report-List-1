<p><p>Hermetic packaging refers to the process of sealing electronic components in airtight enclosures to protect them from external elements. There are various types of hermetic packaging available in the market. Multilayer Ceramic Packages involve stacking ceramic layers to form a sealed package. Pressed Ceramic Packages are made by pressing ceramic material into the desired shape and then hermetically sealing it. Metal Can Packages use metal cans to enclose components. These packaging types ensure improved reliability, longevity, and optimal performance of electronic devices in various industries.</p></p>
