<p><strong>The Hot Runner Temperature Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modular Type</li><li>Non Modular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot runner temperature controllers market can be categorized into two types: modular type and non-modular type. The modular type temperature controllers consist of individual modules that can be easily connected or disconnected to modify the system based on specific requirements. This flexibility allows for customization and efficient management of multiple hot runner systems. On the other hand, the non-modular type temperature controllers come as a complete system that cannot be easily modified or expanded, making them less flexible but often cost-effective for simpler applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Open Gate Hot Runner Systems</li><li>Valve Gate Hot Runner Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot runner temperature controller market finds its application in open gate hot runner systems and valve gate hot runner systems. Open gate hot runner systems are used in processes where the gate remains open during the entire molding cycle, while valve gate hot runner systems use a mechanical valve to control the flow of molten plastic. Both types of systems require temperature controllers to maintain precise and stable temperatures for efficient and high-quality plastic molding processes.</p></p>
