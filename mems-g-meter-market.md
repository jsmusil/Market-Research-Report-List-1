<p><p>MEMS G meter refers to microelectromechanical systems accelerometers that measure gravitational force acting on an object. The market for MEMS G meters is categorized into three types based on the number of axes they measure: 1 axis, 2 axis, and 3 axis. 1 axis G meters measure force in only one direction, such as up and down. 2 axis G meters measure force in two orthogonal directions, typically vertical and horizontal. 3 axis G meters measure forces in all three directions, offering more comprehensive data and allowing for precise motion tracking in applications like smartphones, gaming devices, and navigation systems.</p></p>
