<p><p>The glucose excipient market consists of two main types: anhydrous glucose and glucose monohydrate. Anhydrous glucose refers to glucose that is completely free of water molecules, while glucose monohydrate contains one molecule of water for every molecule of glucose. Both types are widely used in the pharmaceutical and food industry as fillers, binders, and sweeteners. They provide stability to formulations, aid in the manufacturing process, and enhance taste. These glucose excipients play important roles in various products, contributing to their successful development and production.</p></p>
