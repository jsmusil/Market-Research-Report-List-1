<p><p>A Handheld LIBS (Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy) Spectrometer is a portable device that uses laser-induced plasma to analyze the elemental composition of materials. It works by focusing a laser beam onto the surface of a sample, which generates plasma. The emitted light from the plasma is then collected and analyzed to determine the elemental composition of the sample.</p><p>The Handheld LIBS Spectrometer Market is witnessing rapid growth due to its wide range of applications in various industries including metals and alloys, mining, pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and forensics.
