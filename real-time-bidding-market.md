<p><p>Real-time bidding (RTB) market involves the automated buying and selling of online ad inventory. In an open auction market type, ad impressions are made available to multiple buyers, and they bid in real-time to secure the impressions. This competitive process promotes fair pricing and efficiency. In contrast, an invited auction market type restricts participation to a select group of buyers, either through invitation only or exclusive access. This controlled environment allows for more customization and targeting of ad inventory, ensuring higher-quality buyers and potentially higher prices.</p></p>
