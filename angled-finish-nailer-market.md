<p><p>An angled finish nailer is a pneumatic tool used in carpentry and construction to drive angled finish nails into wood or other materials. The nails used with this tool have a smaller head size, which can be easily concealed or covered with putty or wood filler.</p><p>The angled finish nailer market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and precise carpentry tools.
