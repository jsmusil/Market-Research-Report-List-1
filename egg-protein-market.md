<p><p>The Egg Protein market consists of three main types: Whole Egg Powder, Egg White Powder, and Egg Yolk Powder. Whole Egg Powder is made by drying and processing whole eggs into a fine powder, retaining both the egg white and yolk. Egg White Powder is produced by separating the egg whites from the yolks and drying them into a powdered form. On the other hand, Egg Yolk Powder involves extracting the yolks and drying them into a concentrated powder. These powdered forms of eggs are widely used in various food and beverage applications for their high protein content.</p></p>
