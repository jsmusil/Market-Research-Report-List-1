<p><p>RF spectrum and intermodulation analysis refers to the process of examining and analyzing the radio frequency (RF) spectrum to identify and mitigate any interference or distortions that may occur due to intermodulation. Intermodulation occurs when two or more signals within a system combine, resulting in the creation of additional signals at frequencies that are the sum and difference of the original signals. This can lead to interference and degradation of signal quality.</p><p>The RF spectrum and intermodulation analysis market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for efficient wireless communication systems across various industries.
