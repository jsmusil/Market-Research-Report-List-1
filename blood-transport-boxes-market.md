<p><p>The market for blood transport boxes can be broadly categorized into two types: passive cooling and active cooling. Passive cooling blood transportation boxes rely on insulation materials to maintain the desired temperature for the transportation of blood. On the other hand, active cooling blood transportation boxes use refrigeration or cooling systems to actively regulate and control the temperature inside. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient transport of blood products while maintaining their integrity and quality.</p></p>
