<p><p>Photonics Integrated Circuit (IC) market is categorized into three types of integration: monolithic integration, hybrid integration, and module integration. </p><p>1. Monolithic integration refers to the fabrication of all photonic components on a single chip, resulting in a compact and cost-effective design.</p><p>2. Hybrid integration involves integrating multiple photonic chips or components from different semiconductor materials onto a common platform, allowing for greater functionality and performance.</p><p>3. Module integration refers to the integration of fully packaged photonic components or subsystems into a larger system, offering flexibility and scalability for different applications.</p></p>
