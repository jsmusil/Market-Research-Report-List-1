<p><p>The nerve repair biomaterials market involves the use of materials for repairing damaged nerves. Direct nerve repair or neurorrhaphy is a method where the cut ends of a damaged nerve are stitched together using biomaterials to aid in the healing process. Nerve grafting, on the other hand, refers to the use of nerve grafts (segments of nerve tissue) to bridge the gap between two severed nerve ends. Both approaches aim to restore nerve function and promote regeneration, offering potential solutions for patients with nerve injuries.</p></p>
