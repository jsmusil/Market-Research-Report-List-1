Additionally, advancements in technology, such as AI and machine learning, are enhancing the capabilities of smart planting agriculture systems, providing opportunities for market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are shaping its future. One such trend is the integration of Internet of Things (IoT) devices into agriculture.
