<p><p>The handheld portable grinder market consists of three types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic grinders are powered by compressed air and are generally lighter and more compact, making them ideal for applications that require mobility. Electric grinders run on electricity and are easy to use and maintain, making them popular for general-purpose grinding tasks. Hydraulic grinders use hydraulic power and are often used in heavy-duty industrial applications due to their high power and durability. These different types cater to various needs and requirements within the handheld portable grinder market.</p></p>
