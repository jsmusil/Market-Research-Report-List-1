<p><p>Photomask is a crucial component used in manufacturing display devices such as LED, OLED, and others. It acts as a stencil, allowing precise patterning of circuits and pixels during the fabrication process. In LED display market applications, photomasks aid in creating the desired pixel arrangements and patterns. Similarly, for OLED displays, photomasks are used to form organic layers with specific shapes and sizes. In other display technologies, such as LCD or QLED, photomasks help in defining the precise arrangements of liquid crystal cells or quantum dots, respectively.</p></p>
