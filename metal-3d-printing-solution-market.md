<p><p>The Metal 3D Printing Solution market comprises various types of technologies. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) is a technique that uses a high-power laser to melt and fuse metallic powders layer by layer, creating precise 3D objects. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) also uses a high-energy electron beam to melt metal powders, allowing the production of complex structures with excellent mechanical properties. Additionally, other types of metal 3D printing solutions exist, which may incorporate different methods such as binder jetting or material extrusion. These technologies are all aimed at providing efficient and accurate metal additive manufacturing solutions.</p></p>
