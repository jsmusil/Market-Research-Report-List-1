<p><p>Diaphragm smart gas meters refer to innovative gas metering devices that utilize diaphragm technology to measure gas consumption accurately. The market for diaphragm smart gas meters can be categorized into two types: semi-hidden chassis gas meters and embedded chassis gas meters. Semi-hidden chassis gas meters are designed with a partially concealed chassis for aesthetics and easy installation. On the other hand, embedded chassis gas meters are integrated within a building's infrastructure during construction or renovation. These types provide efficient gas measurement and monitoring solutions while minimizing visual impact.</p></p>
