<p><p>Cement siding, also known as fiber cement siding or cementitious siding, is a type of exterior cladding material made from a mixture of cement, cellulose fibers, and other additives. Cement siding offers excellent resistance to termites, rot, and fire, making it a popular choice among homeowners and builders.</p><p>The future outlook for the cement siding market is expected to be promising. The global construction industry is witnessing significant growth, driven by rapid urbanization, population growth, and increasing infrastructure development.
