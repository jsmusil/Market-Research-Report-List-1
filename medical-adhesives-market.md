<p><p>Medical adhesives are used in various healthcare applications such as wound closure, medical device assembly, and drug delivery systems. There are different types of medical adhesives available in the market. Water-based adhesives are non-toxic and have a high bonding strength but require time to dry. Solvent-based adhesives provide instant bonding but may contain harmful chemicals. Solid-based adhesives are pre-mixed and provide excellent flexibility and moisture resistance. Hot melt adhesives are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, offering fast bonding and high resistance to heat and moisture.</p></p>
