<p><p>In the context of lasers and optics, an Output Coupler (OC) is a component used to extract a portion of the laser beam from the laser cavity. Output couplers can come in different shapes or designs to achieve specific effects. Plane output couplers allow a fraction of the laser beam to be transmitted without altering its shape. Concave output couplers have a curved surface that diverges the laser beam. Convex output couplers, on the other hand, focus and converges the laser beam. Each type of output coupler has distinct applications based on the desired output characteristics.</p></p>
