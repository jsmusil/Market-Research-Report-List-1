<p><p>The industrial pneumatic caster market consists of two types: rigid caster and swivel caster. Rigid casters are designed to move in a single direction and provide stability and strength to heavy loads. They are commonly used in applications where straight movement is required, such as towing carts or industrial equipment. On the other hand, swivel casters can rotate 360 degrees, allowing for easy maneuverability and change in direction. They are typically used in applications where flexibility and mobility are essential, such as furniture, dollies, or utility carts.</p></p>
