<p><p>Disposable conductive tips are used in various electronic devices, particularly touchscreen devices, to enhance conductivity and ensure accurate input. Non-filtered conductive tips are simple, cost-effective options that provide basic conductivity without any additional features. They are suitable for general usage. On the other hand, filtered conductive tips are designed with microfilters to reduce fingerprints, smudges, and scratches on screens. These tips offer improved precision and cleanliness, making them ideal for high-precision applications, such as graphic design or medical use. Both types of conductive tips cater to different consumer needs in the market.</p></p>
