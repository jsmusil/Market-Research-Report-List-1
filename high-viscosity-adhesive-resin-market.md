<p><p>High-viscosity adhesive resin market types refer to the different forms in which adhesive resins are available. Water-based adhesives use water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent-based adhesives use organic solvents for bonding. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature but melt when heated for application. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction for bonding. Other types may include pressure-sensitive adhesives or natural resin-based adhesives. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
