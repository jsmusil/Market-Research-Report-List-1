<p><p>Risk management software market types can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted and accessed over the internet, allowing for scalability, flexibility, and remote access. It offers organizations lower upfront cost, easy maintenance, and automatic updates. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and operated on the organization's own physical servers. Both options have their own advantages and organizations can choose the type that best suits their preferences and needs.</p></p>
