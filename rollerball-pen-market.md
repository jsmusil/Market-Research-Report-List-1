<p><p>Rollerball pens are available in two main types: capped and retractable. Capped rollerball pens have a removable cap that protects the pen tip when not in use. These pens are generally seen as more traditional and offer a classic writing experience. On the other hand, retractable rollerball pens have a mechanism to retract the pen tip into the pen body, eliminating the need for a cap. This makes them more convenient as they can be easily carried in pockets or bags without the risk of staining or smudging. Both types cater to different preferences and writing needs.</p></p>
