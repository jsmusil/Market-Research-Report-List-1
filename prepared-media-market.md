Plated media refers to petri dishes containing solid media, which are typically used for colony isolation. Bottled media is liquid media that is dispensed into bottles, commonly used for liquid bacterial cultures. Tubed media refers to agar slants placed in test tubes, suitable for long-term storage. Other types of prepared media could include dip slides, cryovials, or capsules, depending on the specific laboratory requirements.</p></p>
