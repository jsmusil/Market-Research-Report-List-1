<p><p>Pipe pliers are hand tools used to grip and turn pipes and fittings during plumbing or mechanical work. They come in various types, including adjustable inner diameter and fixed inner diameter pliers. Adjustable inner diameter pipe pliers have jaws that can be adjusted to fit different pipe sizes, making them versatile and suitable for various applications. On the other hand, fixed inner diameter pipe pliers have a fixed jaw size and are designed specifically for a particular pipe size. They are more specialized but offer greater stability and durability when working with pipes of a specific diameter.</p></p>
