<p><p>The exploration rig market is categorized into two main types: Heavy Core and Compact Core. Heavy Core rigs are designed for drilling deep and large-diameter core samples in challenging terrains and extreme conditions. They are used in mining and geotechnical exploration projects. On the other hand, Compact Core rigs are smaller in size and ideal for shallow and medium-depth drilling operations. They are commonly utilized in environmental, water, and geothermal drilling projects, where mobility and accessibility are key factors.</p></p>
