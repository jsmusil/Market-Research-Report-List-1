<p><p>Digital paper hygrometers are devices used to measure the humidity levels in the air. There are two types of digital paper hygrometers: absolute and relative. </p><p>Absolute digital paper hygrometers provide an accurate measurement of the actual moisture content in the air. They are commonly used in industrial settings where precise humidity control is required, such as in manufacturing or storage facilities.</p><p>On the other hand, relative digital paper hygrometers provide a measurement of the humidity relative to the current temperature. They are often used in residential or commercial environments to monitor and maintain a comfortable living or working atmosphere.</p></p>
