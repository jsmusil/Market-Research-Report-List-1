<p><p>Internet Protocol (IP) Telephony market can be categorized into three types: softphones, hardware-based, and services market. </p><p>1. Softphones: These are software applications that enable users to make calls over the internet using their computer or mobile devices. They provide features similar to traditional phones such as call forwarding, voicemail, and conferencing.</p><p>2. Hardware-based: This market comprises physical IP phones or devices that connect to the internet to make calls. These devices often have additional features like touchscreens, video calling capabilities, and integration with other communication systems.</p><p>3. Services market: This segment includes service providers that offer IP telephony services to businesses and individuals. They provide the infrastructure, network, and support necessary for making and receiving calls over the internet, often including additional features like virtual phone numbers and call management tools.</p></p>
