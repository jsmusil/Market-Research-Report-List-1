<p><p>Veterinary blood pressure monitors are crucial devices used to measure the blood pressure of animals during medical procedures. The market for these monitors can be categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic monitors are electronic devices that automatically inflate the cuff and measure the blood pressure without any manual intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic monitors require some manual input, such as inflating the cuff, but still provide accurate readings. Both types of blood pressure monitors cater to the specific needs and requirements of veterinary professionals, ensuring efficient and reliable blood pressure measurements in animals.</p></p>
