<p><p>A Quality Management System (QMS) is a set of procedures, policies, and records that ensure consistent quality in products or services. The market for QMS can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise refers to software that is installed and hosted on the company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based QMS is accessible through the internet and hosted on external servers, benefiting from easy scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT infrastructure costs. Both options aim to enhance quality management, but differ in technical implementation and cost structure.</p></p>
