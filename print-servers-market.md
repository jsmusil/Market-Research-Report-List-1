<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>A print server is a device or software that manages printing tasks on a network. Internal print servers are built into printers, allowing them to connect directly to the network without the need for an additional device. External print servers are standalone devices that connect printers to the network. They can be connected via Ethernet or wireless connections and enable multiple printers to be connected and shared by multiple users. Both types of print servers offer convenience, efficiency, and control over printing tasks but differ in their form of integration with the printer.</p></p>
