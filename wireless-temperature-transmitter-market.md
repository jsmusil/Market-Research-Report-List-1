RWB thermal resistance transmitters utilize the principle of a resistance change in metals due to temperature variations, providing accurate temperature measurements. DWB thermocouple transmitters rely on the phenomenon of voltage change at the junction of two different metal wires in response to temperature fluctuations for temperature sensing purposes.
