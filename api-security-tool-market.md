<p><p>API Security Tools are classified into two market types: Cloud-based and On-premises.</p><p>1. Cloud-based market: In this market, API Security Tools are hosted and operated through cloud services. These tools are accessed remotely over the internet, allowing organizations to easily scale their security infrastructure as per their requirements. Cloud-based tools provide convenience, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs since they are managed by the service provider, but they may raise concerns regarding data privacy and dependence on external providers.</p><p>2. On-premises market: In this market, API Security Tools are deployed and managed within the organization's own infrastructure, typically in their data centers. Organizations have full control and ownership of their security tools, ensuring data sovereignty and better compliance with regulatory requirements. However, on-premises tools often require higher upfront costs for hardware and maintenance. They provide greater control and security, but may lack the scalability and convenience offered by cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
