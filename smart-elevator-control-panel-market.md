<p><ul><li>Single Automatic Operation Panel</li><li>Selective Collective Operation Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Elevator Control Panel market consists of two main types: Single Automatic Operation Panel and Selective Collective Operation Panel. Single Automatic Operation Panel is a control panel that allows passengers to select their desired floor and the system automatically directs them to the assigned elevator. On the other hand, Selective Collective Operation Panel is designed to distribute passengers among multiple elevators, optimizing efficiency by grouping passengers going to the same or nearby floors. Both types enhance elevator management, improving convenience and reducing wait times for passengers.</p></p>
