<p><p>Automatic Fare Collection Systems (AFC) are used in public transportation to automate fare collection. The market includes three types: magnetic strip, smart cards, and optical character recognition (OCR). Magnetic strip AFC systems use magnetic strips on tickets to store fare information. Smart card AFC systems use contactless smart cards, which passengers tap on a reader to deduct fare amounts. Optical character recognition AFC systems use cameras to scan and recognize alphanumeric characters on tickets. All three systems aim to streamline ticketing processes and enhance efficiency in public transportation fare collection.</p></p>
