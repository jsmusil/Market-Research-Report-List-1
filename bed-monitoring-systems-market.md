<p><ul><li>Wireless Bed Monitoring Systems</li><li>Wire Bed Monitoring Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bed monitoring systems are used to monitor the movements and activities of patients while they are in bed. There are two types of bed monitoring systems: wireless bed monitoring systems and wired bed monitoring systems. </p><p>Wireless bed monitoring systems utilize sensors placed under the mattress to detect patient movements, sending real-time data to a central monitoring station. This type of system offers flexibility and ease of installation.</p><p>On the other hand, wired bed monitoring systems require physical wired connections between the sensors and the monitoring station. These systems are generally more reliable and suitable for long-term care facilities or hospitals where a higher level of accuracy is required.</p><p>Both types of bed monitoring systems play a crucial role in ensuring patient safety and providing healthcare professionals with valuable data to monitor and respond to any potential risks or emergencies.</p></p>
