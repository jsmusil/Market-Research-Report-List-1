It is commonly used in residential and commercial applications due to its efficient transmission over long distances. On the other hand, DC Supply operates on direct current, where electricity flows consistently in one direction. It is widely used in electronic devices as it ensures stable and reliable power delivery, making it suitable for applications requiring precise control and low noise.</p></p>
