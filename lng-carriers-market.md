<p><p>LNG carriers are ships specifically designed for transporting liquefied natural gas (LNG) from one location to another. There are two main types of LNG carriers: Moss Type (Spherical Storage Tank) and Membrane Type. Moss Type carriers have a spherical storage tank that can withstand high pressures and extreme temperatures, providing a safe way to store LNG. On the other hand, Membrane Type carriers use a flexible membrane system to store LNG, offering increased cargo capacity and overall efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in the global LNG transportation market.</p></p>
