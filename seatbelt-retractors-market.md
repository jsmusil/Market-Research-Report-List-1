<p><p>Seatbelt retractors are an essential component in vehicles to ensure passenger safety. Belt-In-Seat retractors are integrated into the seat itself, offering convenience and improved protection. Six-Point, Five-Point, Four-Point, and Three-Point retractors distribute the forces over multiple points, reducing the risk of injury. Two-Point retractors are the basic type, commonly found in older vehicles, securing the lap only. These various types of seatbelt retractors cater to different requirements and safety standards, providing passengers with options to choose the most suitable and secure belt system for their vehicles.</p></p>
