<p><p>The gas pizza ovens market consists of three types: single-layer ovens, double-layer ovens, and multi-layer ovens. Single-layer ovens are designed to cook one pizza at a time, making them suitable for small establishments or low-demand scenarios. Double-layer ovens have two separate chambers, allowing for two pizzas to be cooked at once, making them more efficient for medium-sized businesses. Multi-layer ovens are equipped with multiple chambers, enabling the simultaneous cooking of multiple pizzas, making them ideal for high-demand environments such as large restaurants or pizzerias. These different types cater to the varying needs and capacities of businesses in the pizza industry.</p></p>
