<p><p>Industrial casters are wheel-mounted devices used to provide mobility to heavy equipment and furniture in industrial settings. There are two main types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters have a rotating mechanism that allows them to turn in any direction, offering increased maneuverability. Rigid casters, on the other hand, only move in a straight line and provide stability and support. These two caster types are designed to cater to different applications and offer distinct benefits, depending on the specific needs of the industrial environment.</p></p>
