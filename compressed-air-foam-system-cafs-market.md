<p><ul><li>Low-expansion Foam System</li><li>Medium-expansion Foam System</li><li>High-expansion Foam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) market includes three types: Low-expansion Foam System, Medium-expansion Foam System, and High-expansion Foam System. Low-expansion foam systems are used in firefighting for extinguishing fires, as they produce a thick foam layer that covers a large area. Medium-expansion foam systems generate a foam layer with higher expansion ratio, suitable for fire suppression in confined spaces. High-expansion foam systems create foam with the highest expansion ratio, making them ideal for filling large spaces with foam, such as warehouses or underground areas, effectively smothering fires.</p></p>
