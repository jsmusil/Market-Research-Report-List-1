<p><p>Subsea trenchers are used in offshore oil and gas industries to excavate trenches on the seabed for various purposes. Mechanical trenchers are a type of subsea trenching equipment that use rotating cutting wheels or chain buckets to dig the seabed. They are suitable for shallow water applications. On the other hand, jet trenchers are subsea trenching machines that utilize high-pressure water jets to remove sediments and create the desired trenches. These are mainly used in deep-water environments where mechanical trenchers may not be practical or efficient.</p></p>
