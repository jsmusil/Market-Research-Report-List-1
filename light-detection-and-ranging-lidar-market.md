<p><p>Light Detection and Ranging (LIDAR) technology is used for measuring distances by illuminating a target with a laser and analyzing the reflected light. There are two main types of LIDAR markets - Airborne LIDAR and Terrestrial LIDAR. Airborne LIDAR involves mounting the sensor on aircraft to capture data from above, while Terrestrial LIDAR involves ground-based sensors for scanning objects or environments from a stationary point. Both markets serve various industries such as mapping, forestry, urban planning, and archaeology.</p></p>
