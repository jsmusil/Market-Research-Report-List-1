Gas Chromatography-Quadrupole Mass Spectrometer employs quadrupole analyzers for accurate and precise mass measurements. Gas Chromatography-Time of Flight Mass Spectrometry uses time-of-flight analyzers to determine mass-to-charge ratios. Gas Chromatography-Ion Trap Mass Spectrometer uses ion traps to trap and analyze ions. These technologies find applications in various industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental, and food and beverages for qualitative and quantitative analysis of compounds.</p></p>
