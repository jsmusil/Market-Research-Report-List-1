<p><p>A Rack and Pinion Elevator is a type of elevator mechanism that uses a rack and pinion system to lift and lower the elevator car. The rack is a toothed rail that is attached to the elevator car, and the pinion is a gear that meshes with the rack and is driven by a motor. As the pinion rotates, it moves the rack, lifting or lowering the elevator car.</p><p>The market for Rack and Pinion Elevators is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The rising demand for vertical transportation in residential and commercial buildings, coupled with the increasing number of high-rise constructions, is driving the market growth.
