<p><p>The drywall texture paint market offers various types of textures for walls. Knockdown texture involves flattening sprayed-on texture with a trowel, resulting in a random pattern. Orange peel texture resembles the textured surface of an orange peel. Popcorn texture creates a bumpy and random pattern, resembling the texture of popcorn. Sand swirl texture involves creating swirl patterns using a sand mixture. Other texture options may include smooth, skip trowel, or comb texture, among others. These different texture options allow customers to personalize and enhance the appearance of their walls.</p></p>
