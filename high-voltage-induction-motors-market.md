<p><strong>The High Voltage Induction Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Squirrel-Cage</li><li>Slip-Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage induction motors market consists of two types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors are the most commonly used and feature a rotor with conductive bars that resemble a squirrel cage. They are known for their simplicity, durability, and low maintenance requirements. Slip-ring motors, on the other hand, have a rotor with insulated coils and external brushes. They offer higher starting torque and are better suited for applications that require frequent starting and stopping. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, such as manufacturing, mining, and infrastructure.</p></p>
