<p><p>Radial ball bearings are commonly used in various industries to reduce friction and support rotational motion. There are different types of radial ball bearings available in the market. Conrad type bearings have a single row of balls and are suitable for moderate loads and speeds. On the other hand, self-aligning type bearings have two rows of balls, allowing them to compensate for misalignment and angular errors. These bearings are commonly used in machines or equipment that experience misalignment due to shaft deflection or mounting errors.</p></p>
